MIX INGREDIENTS ARRANGE ON A BED OF ROMAINE AND POUR OVER THE FOLLOWING DRESSING MIX FOUR TABLESPOONS OLIVE OIL ONE TABLESPOON GRAPE JUICE ONE TABLESPOON GRAPE VINEGAR ONE FOURTH TEASPOON PAPRIKA ONE EIGHTH TEASPOON PEPPER AND ONE TABLESPOON FINELY CHOPPED ROQUEFORT CHEESE. CREAM DRESSING MIX ONE HALF LEVEL TABLESPOON EACH OF SALT AND MUSTARD THREE QUARTERS LEVEL TABLESPOON OF SUGAR ONE EGG SLIGHTLY BEATEN TWO AND ONE HALF TABLESPOONS OF MELTED BUTTER THREE QUARTERS CUP OF CREAM AND HEAT IN A DOUBLE BOILER.
